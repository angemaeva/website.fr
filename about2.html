ABOUT ME

A small description of me

I am Fadeu Miamo Ange Maeva. I was born in 1999 in a small village in the country side of Cameroun called Bafang. During my childhood I loved going to the market with my Aunty to help her selling her groceries , attract clients to her store and make them buy. Each time I did that it brought me some kind of self satisfaction. With time I discovered many other ways of doing it, that is through social networks . My friends and I were very have fun by creating new accounts in every social network, to be known and share our lives with our virtual family. Time passed by and I started managing peoples accounts when they were too busy with life to manage themselves. I created post, interactions with our online friends to maintenant them. Finally I decided to make this childhood passion as my job. That’s how I entered into Digital College in order to specialise myself in Digital Marketing and now I’m able not only to do digital marketing but equally to be a brand strategist , digital communication , community manager, digital brand content and project manager.
