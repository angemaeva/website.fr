Tabou

The powerful freshness of Tabou exhales new sensual and mysterious facets, renewing with magnitude the signature of a composition made a fuck of natural products. The essential oil extracted from the plants, as lively and juicy as ever, invites new spicy notes that give it roundness and sensuality.crossing the freshness of the night, the burning air of the desert exhales deep scents. As the wolves come out and the sky sets on fire, a new magic unfolds. Our team of local perfumers creates each box of perfume with love and dedication.

Why the name tabou? As aggressive as the logo is, it demonstrates a monogram type logo drawn from the letter T of TABOU. The two lines above the logo represent the virility and sensuality of the man. Its simplicity, visibility and scalability allows it to be captivating and imposing.The green color symbolizes the love of nature. It is also a symbol of love for one's family, the bonds of friendship.



Tabou's main target is men between 20-40 years old. The ideal client for Tabou would be a young man of 25 years old, model and photo model who likes to please, to seduce and especially to smell good. His goal is to become a world famous model. With time he hopes to be a man always as sensual and mysterious. He wishes to have a perfume that if perfectly with his personality and charisma. He earns about 5,000 euros per month. The tools he uses the most are his phone and his computer. His favorite social networks are instagram, TIKTOK, Pinterest, and Snapchat.
